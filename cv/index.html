Short Curriculum Vitae

  • Since 2021: PhD under the supervision of Francis Bach and Adrien Taylor in the SIERRA team
  • 2020-2021: Graduated from Ecole des Ponts (Marne-la-Vallée, France) with a Master on public policies for sustainable development
    • Internship at Inspection Générale des Finances: “Accélérer le rebond économique des territoires”, supervised by Paul-Armand Veillon
  • 2019-2020: Graduated from Ecole Normale Supérieure (Saclay, France) with a Master in Maths, Vision and Learning
    • Master thesis at INRIA Paris on “Interplay between optimization and integration methods”, supervised by Francis Bach and Adrien Taylor
  • 2016-2019: Graduated from Ecole Polytechnique (Paris, France) in applied mathematics
    • Master thesis at GERAD on column generation for airline crew rostering, supervised by François Soumis and Issmael El Hallaoui
  • 2013-2016: Scientific preparatory classes

Since 2019, I am an Ingénieure des Ponts, Eaux et Forêts.

Teaching and outreach

  • 02/2023: I co-organized the PEP talks a workshop on Performance Estimation Problems (PEP) with Sébastien Colla and Baptiste Goujaud.
  • 10/2022: I co-organized the RJMI Inria Paris with Bertille Follain, a two-day program to encourage high school girls to study mathematics
  • 2022 & 2021: As a teaching assistant, Introduction to Optimization at Ecole des Ponts, (Frédéric Meunier)

Miscellaneous — I am a regular climber, mostly indoors. I am also vice-president of the Protection Civile Paris 8 and 9, an association that organizes first-aid volunteering and marauding.
